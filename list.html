Lean Startup Machine is a three-day workshop where you will learn how to apply Lean Startup methodologies to build things customers love paying for! No matter where you are in the process, we’ll show you a step-by-step process to develop a product that customers actually want. The process promotes a problem-centric approach to business ideation.
